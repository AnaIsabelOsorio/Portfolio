Coffee Now

CoffeeNow is a digital solution designed to transform customer service in coffee shops by optimizing the ordering and payment process. Through a web platform accessible via unique QR codes at each table, customers can browse the menu, place their orders, and pay directly from their mobile devices, avoiding lines and reducing wait times. The system also supports role-based management for employees, manual order entry, and real-time order tracking in the kitchen.
